About

I always loved video games as a kid. I spent more time than I can remember (probably too much time for someone that age) having Pokemon battles with friends, or trying to beat Super Mario Galaxy. As I grew older, I not only bought more games and consoles of my own, but I also received a number of hand-me-downs from older cousins.

Now, almost two decades after playing my first game, I’ve found myself stuck with a large assortment of old game consoles. I don’t really play video games as much as I used to, but when I do, I usually just go back and play old ones for the nostalgia. So, I do get some occasional use out of these things.

I figured that it would be fun to kind of catalog everything that I have into one place. It gave me the chance to dig up consoles that I haven’t used in years (over a decade in some cases) and just take inventory of everything I’ve got. Hopefully you will find it interesting to look through everything.
