Wii U

Nintendo, 2012

The Wii U was the first Nintendo console to support high-definition (HD) graphics. The primary controller, the Wii U GamePad, features an embedded touchscreen, a D-pad, analog sticks, and action buttons. The screen can be used either as a supplement to the main display or in supported games to play directly on the GamePad. The Wii U is backward compatible with Wii software and accessories.

My Thoughts

My Wii U is probably my most used console ever, and the one that I would probably consider taking with me if there were ever a fire. I mainly just use it to play Wii games, since it's backwards compatible, and it can use an HDMI cord unlike the regular Wii.
