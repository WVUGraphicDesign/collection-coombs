DS Lite

Nintendo, 2006

The Nintendo DS Lite (shortened to DS Lite or NDS Lite) is the second model in the Nintendo DS family and a dual-screen handheld game console developed and manufactured by Nintendo. It is a slimmer, more lightweight redesign of the earlier Nintendo DS model, styled to make it more appealing to a variety of gaming audiences and is much brighter. It was announced on January 26, 2006, more than a month before its first territorial launch in Japan on March 2, 2006, due to overwhelming demand for the original model.
