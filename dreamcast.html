Dreamcast

Sega, 1999

The Dreamcast is the final home video game console manufactured by Sega. It was released in Japan on November 27, 1998, and worldwide in late 1999. It succeeded the Sega Saturn and was the first sixth-generation console, preceding Sony's PlayStation 2, Nintendo's GameCube, and Microsoft's Xbox.

My Thoughts

This console only has a few good games, but the style of it is so unique that it's always been one of my favorites to own. Also, being able to use the fishing rod controller on Sega Bass Fishing is a huge plus.
