GBA SP

Nintendo, 2003

The Game Boy Advance SP (SP stands for "Special") is a 32-bit handheld game console made by Nintendo. It was released in Japan on February 14, 2003, and in international markets in March. The SP is an upgraded version of the Game Boy Advance with a more compact clamshell design.

My Thoughts

I remember buying this from a store downtown (now closed) called Vintage Video Games. I walked down there from my house with my best friend, and bought it for $8 (it now goes for around $110.) I got a lot of use out of this thing, and will probably use it again.
